Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content.The editor supports manangement of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images,videos,and Markdown.Extensiblelity with plugins and themes provide easy way to add functionality or change the looks of a blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts.Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories,sections,format,or flow.With this functionality,you're in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliablility and speed in mind,worldwide data centers provide the backbone for ultra-fast connectivity.This ensures your site will load instantly,no matter where your readers are,keeping your site competitive.

Free,open,simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by a large communication of helpful developers.It supports features such as code syntax highlighting,RSS feed,social media integration,third-party commenting tools,and works seamlessly with Google Analytics.The architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.

Powerful tooling

Batteries included.We build a sample and straightforward CLI tool that makes customization and development a breeze,but capable of producing even the most complicated sites.
